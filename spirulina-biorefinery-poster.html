Circular Economy Loop · BSFL Bioconversion
Black Soldier Fly Larvae (BSFL) Bioconversion
Feed remaining organics to BSFL — one of nature's most efficient bioconverters. Larvae transform low-value material into two sellable products. Conversion ratio ~1.7 kg input → 1 kg larvae. Cycle time: 14–18 days.

Products

Insect protein meal (~40% protein) — feed / aquaculture
Insect fat / lipid — animal feed or biodiesel
Frass fertilizer — NPK-rich, soil-microbiome active (~60% yield)
Chitin extract — biopesticide / medical biopolymer
Live prepupae — exotic animal / reptile / bird live feed

Final Stage — Energy Recovery
Biogas Production via Anaerobic Digestion
Any remaining organic matter enters anaerobic digestion — generating renewable energy and a liquid digestate that loops back as fertilizer. Closes the cascade to near-zero waste and qualifies for renewable energy incentives.

Products

Biomethane (CH₄) — renewable heat & power for facility
Liquid digestate — loops back as liquid fertilizer input
CO₂ (captured) — sell to greenhouses or carbonation market
Biochar (pyrolysis variant) — carbon credits + soil amendment
